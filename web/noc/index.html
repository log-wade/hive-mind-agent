How it works

One orchestrator runs on a schedule and on webhook. It reads your systems, decides, and acts—or escalates with clear instructions.

01

Ingest

Pulls open alerts and metrics from DCIM, BMS, SCADA, and ticketing via your existing APIs.

02

Triage

Correlates by asset and time, applies playbooks, and chooses: remediate via API or escalate to physical work.

03

Act

Acknowledges alerts, runs allowed procedures (resets, setpoints), creates or updates tickets, and notifies when humans need to step in.
